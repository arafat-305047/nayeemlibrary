Our Story

The IIS Library is the knowledge hub of the Institute of Information Science (IIS) of Noakhali Science & Technology University,committed to supporting academic excellence, research, and lifelong learning. Our collection spans a wide range of subjects including science, technology, literature, and beyond—designed to serve students, faculty, and researchers alike.

We offer a blend of traditional and digital resources, including books, journals, e-books, databases, and a modern reading environment. With a user-friendly catalog system and dedicated library staff, the IIS Library ensures seamless access to information and learning tools.
Whether you're working on a research project, preparing for exams, or simply exploring new ideas, the IIS Library is here to support your intellectual journey.
